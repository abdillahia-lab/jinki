Industries We Serve

Aerial Intelligence
Across Every Vertical

From critical infrastructure to precision agriculture — our platform adapts to deliver the intelligence each industry demands.

Infrastructure Inspection

45% cost reduction McKinsey Global Institute

Autonomous aerial intelligence replaces manual inspection of power lines, bridges, pipelines, and solar farms — delivering millimeter-precision defect data in a fraction of the time.

  • Reduce inspection cycles from weeks to hours with autonomous flight planning
  • Detect sub-centimeter structural defects invisible to ground-level assessment
  • Generate automated compliance reports with geotagged anomaly data
  • Eliminate confined-space and at-height safety risks for inspection teams
Managed Advantage

Regulatory complexity around BVLOS (Beyond Visual Line of Sight) operations is real — and it's exactly where our FAA-certified operations team and CISSP-credentialed data handling give clients a turnkey advantage. We manage the airspace, you receive the intelligence.

Data Center Monitoring

70% fewer unplanned outages Gartner

Thermal aerial intelligence detects HVAC failures, hot spots, and facility anomalies before they cascade into downtime — integrating directly with your DCIM and BMS systems.

  • Identify thermal anomalies across rooftop infrastructure in minutes, not days
  • Predict cooling system failures with AI-driven trend analysis
  • Monitor physical security perimeters and detect unauthorized access
  • Generate facility health reports that integrate with existing DCIM platforms
Managed Advantage

Data privacy and airspace restrictions near facilities are critical considerations. Our SOC 2-compliant, end-to-end encrypted data pipeline and controlled airspace coordination protocols ensure zero data exposure and full regulatory compliance.

Precision Agriculture

Up to 25% yield improvement PwC / McKinsey

Multispectral aerial intelligence transforms crop management — from early disease detection to irrigation optimization — enabling data-driven decisions across every acre.

  • Generate NDVI and multispectral vegetation indices across entire properties
  • Detect crop stress, disease, and pest damage weeks before visible onset
  • Optimize irrigation with precision water-stress mapping
  • Build season-over-season predictive yield models with historical aerial data
Managed Advantage

Initial technology adoption cost is a common concern. Our analysis shows aerial intelligence typically delivers positive ROI within a single growing season through reduced input waste, earlier intervention, and improved yield prediction accuracy.

Security & Surveillance

60% faster threat response Deloitte

Autonomous aerial patrols deliver persistent surveillance for corporate campuses, critical infrastructure, and high-value facilities — augmenting security teams with intelligent overwatch.

  • Deploy autonomous patrol routes covering areas impossible for ground teams
  • Detect and classify intrusions with AI-powered real-time analytics
  • Integrate alerts directly with existing SIEM and SOC workflows
  • Reduce security personnel costs while expanding coverage area by 10x
Managed Advantage

Privacy compliance and regulatory considerations are foundational to our approach. Every deployment follows strict data minimization principles, and our AI Governance-certified (AIGP) leadership ensures responsible, compliant surveillance that protects both our clients and the communities they operate in.
