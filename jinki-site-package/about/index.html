About Jinki

The Intelligence Layer
Above Your Operations

We build autonomous aerial intelligence systems that transform how organizations see, understand, and protect their most critical assets.

Our Mission

Trusted Aerial Intelligence
for Critical Operations

Jinki Aerial Intelligence exists to give organizations a decisive informational advantage over the assets they protect, maintain, and grow. We combine certified security expertise, autonomous aerial platforms, and applied AI to transform raw flight data into actionable intelligence — delivered with the rigor and accountability that critical infrastructure demands.

Most aerial operators sell flight time. We built an intelligence company that uses aerial platforms as sensors, not products — fusing computer vision, geospatial analytics, and domain expertise to turn raw captures into decisions our clients can act on the same day. The organizations we serve don't need more footage. They need verified answers.

Founder & CEO

Our founder brings a rare combination of deep security credentials and operational discipline to aerial intelligence. Holding both the CISSP and CCSP certifications from ISC2, he applies the same frameworks that protect enterprise networks and cloud environments to safeguarding the sensitive data Jinki collects over client facilities — an assurance most aerial service providers simply cannot offer.

As an IAPP-certified AI Governance Professional (AIGP), he ensures that every AI model Jinki deploys is governed with the transparency, fairness, and accountability that regulated industries require. His PMI Project Management Professional (PMP) credential reflects a career spent delivering complex, multi-stakeholder technology programs on schedule and within scope — exactly the rigor you want behind the team operating above your most critical assets.
