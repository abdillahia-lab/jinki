Data centers are the backbone of the digital economy. A single outage can cost millions in lost revenue and SLA penalties. Traditional monitoring covers internal systems well, but the building envelope — roofing, HVAC units, electrical infrastructure — often goes unmonitored until something fails.

## The Aerial Advantage

Autonomous aerial systems equipped with radiometric thermal cameras can survey an entire data center campus in minutes. The resulting thermal maps reveal heat signatures invisible to ground-level observation: overloaded transformers, failing HVAC compressors, compromised roofing insulation, and electrical faults.

## Predictive, Not Reactive

Our AI models are built to analyze thermal data against historical baselines, identifying trends before they reach critical thresholds. A transformer running significantly above its seasonal average gets flagged before failure — giving operations teams time to schedule maintenance proactively.

## Integration with Existing Systems

Jinki's platform is designed to integrate with DCIM and BMS systems, feeding aerial intelligence into the monitoring dashboards your team already uses. The goal: no new tools to learn, no workflows to change — just a new layer of visibility.
