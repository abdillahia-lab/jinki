## ROUND 1: LANDING PAGE HERO

### Team Alpha — "Cinematic Command Center" (1,200 lines)
**Approach**: Dual canvas system (particle field + data streams), 4 parallax layers, typewriter headline with word-by-word reveal, HUD corner decorations, magnetic CTA buttons with pulse rings, stats ticker, vignette overlay.

**Strengths**:
- Canvas-based particle system is genuinely impressive (infrastructure-shaped particles)
- 4-layer parallax depth creates cinematic feel
- Typewriter effect with per-word stagger timing is smooth
- Magnetic button effect adds premium tactile feel
- 15 ARIA attributes, 2 reduced-motion blocks, 23 light theme overrides

**Weaknesses**:
- Dual canvas could hurt mobile performance
- 1,200 lines for a hero is heavy — maintenance concern

### Team Omega — "Topographic Intelligence Map" (1,153 lines)
**Approach**: SVG topographic contour map with stroke-dashoffset draw-on-load, split-screen before/after reveal, floating HUD metric cards with tilt, word-swap cycling headline, radar scan line, ambient particle canvas.

**Strengths**:
- SVG topographic map is thematically perfect for an aerial intelligence company
- Split-reveal is a unique concept — dramatic entrance
- Word-swap headline ("Act Before It Fails" → "Know Before It Breaks" → "Fix Before It Costs") adds content dynamism
- Floating HUD metric cards reinforce the intelligence/data theme
- Scan line is a brilliant brand-consistent touch

**Weaknesses**:
- 11 ARIA attributes (fewer than Alpha)
- Split reveal may cause layout shift on slow connections
